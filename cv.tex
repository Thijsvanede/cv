	\section{Research Interests}
		My current research focuses on the application of large language models (LLMs) for the automated analysis and mitigation of security vulnerabilities.
		In the past, I concentrated on network-based approaches for constructing and analysing detection mechanisms and on developing AI systems for automated analysis of security events.
